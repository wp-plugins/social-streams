Collect, curate and display social media content from Twitter and Instagram directly on your WordPress website.

== Description ==

[Social Streams](https://socialstreams.com/) lets you aggregate and display social media content from Instagram and Twitter directly on your WordPress website. Collect conversations and photos from your own social content or public conversations based on hashtags - all in one place. Social Streams is focused on simplicity and making photography shine.

Features:

- Streams Twitter and Instagram
  - Stream your own Twitter & Instagram feeds or public feeds using hashtags
- Simple and easy to use and manage.
  - This is the most straightforward social plugin available. You’ll be up and running in minutes.
  - Auto-Approve mode allows new tweets and ‘grams to publish immediately
  - Or manage content using our super simple moderation panel from your mobile or desktop
  - Automagically integrates with your theme and is fully responsive. Social Streams will pick up your fonts and link colors (and can be hacked further with CSS)
- Full-Page Event Stream
  - Social Streams also gives you a live-updating, full-screen Social Stream to display at your performances and events or in your stores, restaurants and venues.
- Shortcode Integration
  - Display Social Streams anywhere on your site by using the [social-streams] shortcode. 
- Stunning. Yes it’s a feature.
  - Displays all your social content in an aesthetically pleasing grid.
  - Focused on making images and videos look fantastic
- Smart integration with WordPress
  - seamless, responsive, full-page feed with no code required
  - Great for SEO as all social content is directly on the page .
  - No code required, but Social Streams can be hacked with CSS for look and feel.
  - When you publish your Stream it creates a page for you which you can add to your navigation, change the title or add content above the stream as you wish.

== Installation ==

1. Add **Social Streams** through your Admin Panel or Upload the ZIP folder from Wordpress.org
1. Activate the plugin in Wordpress admin screen.
1. Click the Social Streams menu item in your WordPress Admin area
1. Connect your Twitter and/or Instagram accounts
1. Preview some hashtags or stream your own content
1. When you’re ready, hit PUBLISH!
